About
Legend of the Samurai is a FREE interactive application for Android, Windows Phone and Web. This application details the real lives of the samurai, based on historical evidence.

The samurai have for too long been shrouded in myth and mystery, even though much has been known about their actual lives and culture.

This application is only intended to educate, and entertain the reader.
